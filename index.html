About the Big Ten Conference

The Big Ten Conference is one of the most storied and competitive leagues in college football, featuring powerhouse programs across the Midwest and beyond. Known for passionate fan bases, historic rivalries, and a tradition of excellence both on and off the field, the Big Ten continues to shape the landscape of NCAA athletics. From legendary stadiums like the Big House and the Horseshoe to new additions on the West Coast, the conference showcases some of the best talent in the nation.
