About the Big Ten

The Big Ten Conference is one of the oldest and most competitive college football leagues in the United States. Known for its passionate fan bases, historic rivalries, and powerhouse programs, the Big Ten features some of the toughest competition in the country. With recent expansion, the conference now includes schools from coast to coast, but the tradition of hard-nosed football and packed stadiums remains the same.
