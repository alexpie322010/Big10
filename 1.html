Penn State vs Iowa - Pregame Breakdown

Published: October 15, 2025

Penn State travels to Iowa City this weekend to take on the Hawkeyes in what promises to be one of the toughest tests of the season. Both teams come in with a lot on the line: pride, rankings, and momentum heading deeper into October.

The Lions, still reeling from coaching changes and quarterback adjustments, have leaned heavily on their defense. While critics doubt their offensive depth, the coaching staff believes Ethan Grunkemeyer is ready to step up in the absence of Allar. His preparation and poise during practice sessions have become a major talking point among fans.

Iowa, on the other hand, brings its trademark physicality and home-field advantage at Kinnick Stadium. With Mark at the helm, their offense is steady if unspectacular, but it’s the defense that continues to anchor the Hawkeyes’ success. Playing under the lights, Iowa has historically thrived in these primetime battles.

Analysts expect a close, grind-it-out game with limited scoring. Oddsmakers currently favor Penn State slightly, but most agree it’s a coin flip. Whoever wins the battle in the trenches will likely leave victorious. Special teams play is also expected to be a decisive factor.

For fans, the storylines extend beyond the field: Can Penn State find stability under interim leadership? Will Iowa defend its home turf once again? With everything at stake, this clash may define the second half of the Big Ten season.
