Penn State vs Iowa: Pregame, Odds, and Key Storylines

Published: October 15, 2025

The Penn State Nittany Lions will face off against the Iowa Hawkeyes on October 18th at 4:00 PM ET in Kinnick Stadium. This matchup comes at a critical time for both teams as conference standings begin to tighten.

Penn State enters the game with questions at quarterback. With Drew Allar sidelined for the season, Ethan Grunkemeyer steps in as starter. His poise under pressure will be closely watched as fans wonder if he can keep Penn State’s offense rolling.

On the Iowa side, quarterback Mark Anderson has been steady but not spectacular. The Hawkeyes will lean on their stout defense and disciplined play, especially at home in a raucous Kinnick environment.

Betting lines currently favor Penn State by a slim +3. This indicates what fans already know — the game will be close, likely decided by turnovers or one explosive play late in the fourth quarter.

Fans across the Big Ten are eagerly awaiting this clash. It’s not just about Penn State or Iowa — the outcome will ripple across the conference standings and could shape the road to Indianapolis.
